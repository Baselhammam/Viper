# AcmeCorp WidgetPro X1 — User Manual

## Overview

The WidgetPro X1 is a wireless smart home device that connects via Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz / 5 GHz)
and Bluetooth 5.0. It is designed for home automation, scheduling, and remote monitoring.

---

## Package Contents

- WidgetPro X1 unit
- USB-C charging cable (1 m)
- Wall mount bracket + screws
- Quick-start guide

---

## Getting Started

### First-Time Setup

1. Download the **AcmeCorp Home** app from your device's app store (iOS or Android).
2. Press and hold the **Power** button for 3 seconds until the LED ring turns **blue**.
3. In the app tap **Add Device → WidgetPro X1** and follow the on-screen prompts.
4. The device reboots once automatically when setup finishes.

---

## Daily Operation

### Power Controls

| Action | Method | LED Colour |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Power on | Press Power once | Green |
| Power off | Hold Power 2 s | Red, then off |
| Sleep mode | Double-tap Power | Faint white pulse |

### Adjusting Settings

Open the AcmeCorp Home app → **Devices → WidgetPro X1 → Settings**.

| Setting | Default | Range |
|---------|---------|-------|
| Brightness | 70 % | 0–100 % |
| Auto-off timer | 30 min | Off / 5–120 min |
| Motion sensitivity | Medium | Low / Medium / High |

## Troubleshooting

### Device Won't Connect to Wi-Fi

1. Confirm your router broadcasts on 2.4 GHz (the X1 does not support 5 GHz during setup).
2. Move the device within 5 metres of the router.
3. Restart both the router and the WidgetPro X1.
4. If the problem persists, perform a **factory reset** (see below).

### LED Blinking Red Rapidly

This means a firmware update is in progress. **Do not power off the device.**
The update takes 2–5 minutes; the device reboots automatically when complete.

### Unresponsive / Frozen Device

Hold the **Reset** pinhole button (on the underside) for 5 seconds using a paperclip.
The device will force-restart without erasing your settings.

---

## Factory Reset

> **Warning:** A factory reset erases all settings and removes the device from your account.
> You will need to re-add it in the app afterwards.

1. Press and hold both the **Power** and **Sync** buttons simultaneously for **8 seconds**.
2. The LED ring flashes white **three times**, then turns off.
3. The device restarts in pairing mode (blue LED).
4. Re-add it using the AcmeCorp Home app.
